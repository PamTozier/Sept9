What’s the big Ordeal?

The “Oskar Blues Ordeal” bus trips during GABF week offer GABF-goers and craft beer lovers the chance to go backstage at all of our locations and check out what we’re all about.

We’re throwing Ordeal events Wednesday, October 9th through Friday, October 11th.
